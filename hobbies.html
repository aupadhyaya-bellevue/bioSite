Cooking
Anupama has been cooking for a very long time and now it has become her most favorite hobby. She loves to cook for friends and family. Her specialties are mostly in Indian cuisine but also loves explore new recipes and experiment with some already known ones. She also recently started this as her profession and now run her own business of home cooked food. She wants to take this to the next level and open her own restaurant.

Travel
Travelling and exploring new places and food is something not only her hobby but also her passion. She loves to go to new places and try various cuisines and variety of foods.
