About: Anupama Upadhyaya

Anupama was born in Siddhharthnagar and city in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India. Siddharthnagar is located on India-Nepal border 32 miles from Lumbini, the birthplace of Gautam Buddha. She is graduated in arts from India and came to US about a decade ago. Cooking is her hobby, and she is really good in that. She started this as a hobby by trying different recipes and later started adding her own flavor to various Indian cuisine dishes. She has been cooking since she was very young and now is expert of many dishes. Her some of the dishes like mix veg curry that’s various vegetables cooked in mild creamy gravy with very little of some Indian spices, chicken curry – chicken with bone cooked with loads of spices are really yummy and most favorite of all of us. She also recently started her small business as home food service for others and have been very successful there as well. She also manages some social media pages on Facebook as well as Instagram and great comments and recommendations from some people who ordered food from her are very evident about her cooking skills and her knowledge of Indian cuisine. She mainly cooks North Indian food mostly from northern part of India but she is equally good in southern Indian dishes as well. She is mother of two and other than cooking loves to travel. She also is fond of movies that she does most of the time when have some free time.
